What the colors mean

Each data point about a submission is colorized.
Green hues are meant to convey "newness", or "undiscoveredness" (if thats even a word).
Red and orange are meant to convey something that is popular.

Lime Green

  • Points: less than 10
  • Submitter: has a karma score less than 10% of users amoung same registration month
  • Age of Submission: less than 30 minutes
  • Comments: less than 10

Green

  • Points: less than 50
  • Submitter: has a karma score less than 30% of users amoung same registration month
  • Age of Submission: less than 1 hour old
  • Comments: less than 20

Orange

  • Points: less than 100
  • Submitter: has a karma score less than 50% users amoung same registration month
  • Age of Submission: less than 6 hours old
  • Comments: less than 100

OrangeRed

  • Points: less than 500
  • Submitter: Not used
  • Age of Submission: less than 12 hours old
  • Comments: less than 300

Red

  • Points: less than 1000
  • Submitter: has a karma score lower than 80% users amoung same registration month
  • Age of Submission: less than 24 hours old
  • Comments: less than 900

DarkRed

  • Points: less than 2000
  • Submitter: has a karma score lower than 95% users amoung same registration month
  • Age of Submission: less than 48 hours old
  • Comments: less than 1500

Black

  • Points: over 2000
  • Submitter: has a karma score higher than 95% users amoung same registration month
  • Age of Submission: over 48 hours old
  • Comments: over 1500
